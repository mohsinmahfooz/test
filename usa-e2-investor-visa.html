Requirements for USA E2 Investor Visa

The E2 Visa is available to nationals of countries that hold a treaty of commerce and navigation with the United States, including Canada, Grenada, Türkiye, and the United Kingdom. It allows eligible entrepreneurs to enter the US to develop, direct, and manage a business in which they have made a substantial investment. Legal Basis

The E2 Visa program is established under paragraph 101(a)(15)(E) of the US Immigration and Nationality Act (INA). This legislation defines the criteria for granting non-immigrant visas to nationals of treaty countries who wish to enter the United States to develop and direct an enterprise in which they have made a substantial capital investment. The US government determines, through applicable regulations, what constitutes a substantial investment qualifying for E2 status.
