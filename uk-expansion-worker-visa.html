What are the Requirements?

Requirements for UK Expansion Worker Visa Program

The UK Expansion Worker Visa falls under the Global Business Mobility category of the UK's Points-Based Immigration System. It is designed for employees of active overseas businesses who are relocating to the UK to set up a branch or subsidiary. Applicants must be sponsored by a UK-licensed organisation and must hold a senior managerial or specialist role within the business. Unlike the Innovator Founder Visa, applicants are not starting their own business but expanding an existing one.

  • 18 years of age or older
  • Hold a valid passport
  • No criminal record
  • Employment in an eligible managerial or specialist role within an active overseas business
  • Successfully pass due diligence and background checks
  • Tuberculosis test results if applying from a listed country (e.g. Afghanistan, China, India)
  • Assigned a Certificate of Sponsorship (CoS) by a UK-licensed sponsor organisation
  • Minimum annual salary of £48,500

Eligible Dependents

  • Spouse of the main applicant
  • Biological or adopted dependent children

LEGAL BASIS

LEGAL BASIS

The UK Expansion Worker Visa is established under the UK Immigration Rules within the Points-Based System, specifically under the Global Business Mobility category. Individuals fulfilling the requirements of this category may apply to establish their overseas employer's presence in the United Kingdom. The visa is administered by UK Visas and Immigration (UKVI) and is legally grounded within the Immigration Rules under Part 5 of the Points-Based System framework.
