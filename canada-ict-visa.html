What are the Benefits?

Benefits of Canada ICT Visa

The ICT Visa provides a comprehensive pathway for business owners and their families to build a new life and business presence in Canada.

Permanent Residency and Citizenship

After living in Canada for three years you can apply for permanent residency, and after five years you become eligible for full Canadian citizenship and a highly respected passport.

World-Class Health and Education

Canada's healthcare and education systems are renowned globally. As a permanent resident, you and your children enjoy access to free public healthcare and public schooling.

Include Your Entire Family

Your spouse or common-law partner and children under 22 years of age can all be included in your application, ensuring your whole family benefits from this pathway.

Sponsor Key Employees

As a business owner operating in Canada, you have the ability to sponsor senior employees from your parent company to support the operations of your Canadian entity.

Access to North American Markets

The ICT program opens the door to both the Canadian and US markets, with a stable banking environment, strong financing options, and abundant business growth opportunities.

Dual Citizenship Permitted

Canada allows dual citizenship, so you can retain your existing nationality while building your life and business in Canada, without any obligation to notify your home country.

What are the Requirements?

Requirements for Canada ICT Visa

Canada's Intra-Company Transfer program is an immigration pathway under the International Mobility Program for qualified foreign business owners who wish to expand into Canada. Applicants must establish a qualifying relationship between their existing overseas company and a new Canadian entity, and must have held a managerial, executive, or specialised knowledge role within that company for at least one year. RK Global Immigration manages every aspect of the setup and application process.

  • 18 years of age or older
  • Good health with no serious medical conditions
  • No criminal record
  • Successfully pass due diligence and background checks
  • Meet the specific eligibility criteria of the selected Talent Passport category
  • At least one year of experience in a managerial, executive, or specialised knowledge role
  • Confirmed and documented investment sources (minimum net worth of C$200,000 recommended)

Eligible Dependents

  • Spouse of the main applicant
  • Biological or adopted children under 22 years of age

LEGAL BASIS

LEGAL BASIS

The Canada Intra-Company Transfer Visa falls under the International Mobility Program, governed by the Immigration and Refugee Protection Act (IRPA) and its associated regulations. It facilitates the temporary entry of intra-company transferees holding managerial, executive, or specialised knowledge positions within a qualifying multinational company. The Canadian branch must demonstrate an active and genuine business relationship with the parent company abroad.
