About Turkey

Turkey is a transcontinental G20 nation of over 85 million people, strategically positioned between Europe, the Middle East, and Central Asia. Its dynamic economy, vibrant real estate market, and extensive bilateral agreements make it one of the most commercially significant and geopolitically connected nations in the world.

Turkey's E-2 treaty with the United States provides a distinct pathway to US business residency, setting it apart from other non-Caribbean CBI programmes.

Requirements for TURKEY

Requirements for TURKEY Citizenship by Investment. Applicants must be at least 18 years old, have a clean criminal record, and demonstrate a verifiable and lawful source of funds for the qualifying investment.

Legal Basis

Turkey's programme is governed by Article 12 of Turkish Citizenship Law No. 5901 and administered by the General Directorate of Population and Citizenship Affairs. RK Global Immigration ensures full compliance with all applicable legal frameworks in the UAE and Turkey, coordinating with authorised Turkish legal professionals throughout the process.
